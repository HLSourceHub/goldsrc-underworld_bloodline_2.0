ASSAULT AND RESCUE!

Objective: Vampires have imprisoned the Hybrid
deep within a factory complex. Lycans must
assault the building to rescue the Hybrid and
help him escape through the cargo elevator.

Use E to activate door locks, and to capture or
release the Hybrid.

Scoring: Lycans gain 5 points for unlocked doors
and 15 for rescuing the Hybrid, any team looses
5 points for killing him.
