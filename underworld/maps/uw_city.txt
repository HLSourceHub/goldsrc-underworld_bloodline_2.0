HUNT THE HYBRID!

Objective: Your team must hypnotize a Hybrid and lead
him to the prison cell at your base. Use E to pick up
or use objects, and to capture or release a Hybrid.

Scoring: Gain 10 points for capturing a Hybrid, but
loose 5 points if you kill him.

Locations:
- Vampire cell is on the upper floor of the mansion.
- Lycan cell is at the end of the subway tunnel.

Note: Vampires leap higher and Lycans move faster in
superhuman mode. Hold down SHIFT key to activate.



MOVEMENT CONTROLS
Mouse - look
W - move forward
S - move back
A - move left
D - move right
CTRL - duck
SPACE - jump
CTRL+SPACE - high jump

WEAPON CONTROLS
Mouse 1 - fire/attack
Mouse 2 - secondary attack
R - reload
[ - next weapon
] - previous weapon
Q - last used weapon
1-5 - select weapon

MENU CONTROLS
N - change team
B - change character
M - other options

TEAM CONTROLS
Y - chat
U - team chat
TAB - view scores
