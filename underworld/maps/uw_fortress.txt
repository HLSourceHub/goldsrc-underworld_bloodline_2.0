CAPTURE THE HYBRID!

Objective: Invade the enemy Fortress and rescue the
Hybrid from the "Holding Cell" in the Tower. Lead
the Hybrid back to your base and into your "Capture
Cell". Use E to open or close cell doors, and to
capture or release a Hybrid.

Scoring: Gain 20 points for capturing a Hybrid, but
loose 5 points if you kill him.

Note: Vampires leap higher and Lycans move faster in
superhuman mode. Hold down SHIFT key to activate.
